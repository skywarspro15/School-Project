About current project: The Timeless Voyage: A Journey to Ancient China

What happens when a person was teleported back to the ancient?


Well, in that case, it happened.

Meet Charly, a young person who enjoys history. He frequently visits the library to obtain books about history.

Until one day... He woke up out of nowhere. He was confused, and looked around the surroundings. He discovers that he is in the Ancient Chinese times.

Watch as Charly goes through a journey to explore, and conquer Ancient China. Would he succeed? Let's find out.
