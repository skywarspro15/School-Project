About the LifeLabs project

The LifeLabs project is a group project by Tranch Research and Fusion Cinema.

Each quarter, we make different content on this site, and for this quarter, we present to you the Indonesian shadow puppets called Wayang Kulit.

The storyline is about the Ancient Chinese civilization, with their journey to grow and expand.

We have hand curated songs from Indonesia's Gamelan music to provide the most cinematic experience out of a puppet show.
