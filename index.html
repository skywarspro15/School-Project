The Storyboard

  • The Backstory

    Once upon a time, There was a young girl called Xanaya. At the moment, She's financially unstable so she's been having difficulties surviving.

  • Love at First Sight?

    One day, she was searching for food not until she bumped into a young man called Haze. In Love Stories, many call this “Love at First Sight” and it seems to be the same thing to both Xanaya and Haze.

  • The Offering

    And that's where their deep conversation started. As Haze started to realize Xanaya's problem, he offered to help Xanaya until she gets financially stable.

  • The Refusal

    And of course, Xanaya said: "Oh, no need Haze. It's embarrassing for someone to ask if they can help me especially when we just met."

  • The Persuasion

    Of course, haze didn't give up and tried to persuade her again. "It's no problem for me. I promise to make things easier for you but if you really don't want to then I'll respect that." Haze said.

  • Second Thoughts

    Xanaya had second thoughts. With her situation, she was unable to eat for some days and if those days keep coming, she won't be able to survive.

  • The Agreement

    With that, she decided to agree with the offer that haze proposed. And of course, haze was happy not only because he had feelings towards her but because he will be able to help her.

  • Is this love?

    Turns out that Haze was actually true about his words! He was able to help Xanaya. Xanaya was able to eat regularly now and sometimes, haze will give her gifts like jewelry and clothes. Because of that, Xanaya's feelings towards Haze grew.

  • The Confession

    Both Xanaya and Haze confessed their feelings for each other months ago and now Haze is planning to propose to Xanaya.

  • The Big Yes

    It was a Sunday Evening and Haze prepared a dinner date with Xanaya. He proposed and Xanaya said a big yes.

  • The Antagonist Comes In

    The couple was happy not until a man interrupted their night at their house. "Oh there you are, I have been looking for you ever since you stole my treasures!"

  • The Shock

    Haze was speechless while Xanaya was confused. Since it was night and Xanaya wanted no harm, she said: " I'm sorry sir but we don't know what you're talking about, I think it's best for you to go home since it's quite late now."

  • The Antagonist's Introduction

    The antagonist proceeded with: "Call me Ling! And for your information, YOU don't know what I'm talking about but your man pretty sure knows what I'm saying."

  • The Confused Scare

    Xanaya turned to Haze and gave him a confused yet scared look. Haze wanted all this to end.

  • Antagonist Gets Kicked Out

    "I'm sorry Sir Ling but I don't know what you're talking about so pls get out. Haze did not hesitate to push him out of the house." Haze said.

  • Something Comforting

    After that, he went to Xanaya to comfort her. "Don't mind him, he's crazy. Let's sleep now and forget about this okay"?

  • The Suspicion

    Xanaya nodded and both of them slept again. It has been a few days since that night happened and Xanaya felt like something will happen on that day but she just shrugged it off.

  • The Assassination

    She was right. It was late afternoon, The couple were talking not until . . . "How bold of him to push me away right after he stole things at my home !? Now I'll give him a taste of his own medicine." Ling says. With that, He angled the arrow to Haze and then it hits him.

  • The Final Goodbye

    "I love you..." This was Haze's last words before his death.
    "Stay with me please, I can't lose you. Don't leave me like how they left me, you're the only person I have!" Xanaya. said.

  • The Sad Ending

    Xanaya was left with unanswered questions and one of those is the death of her lover. Oh how she wished that she could turn back time and see him again but she knows that it's too late. And that's the end of her Tragic Love Story.

Not all stories must end in a happy ending.

Sometimes, stories end in a somewhat tragic way. After all, that's life. There's no such thing as a "happy ever after".
Although, do you want her to have a happy ending? Let us know! We might just create a sequel!

About the LifeLabs project

The LifeLabs project is a group project by Tranch Research Institute.

Our goal for this project is to deliver information in a way people could understand.
Each quarter, we make different content on this site, and for this quarter, we present to you the Indonesian shadow puppets called Wayang Kulit.

The storyline is about a love story that turned into tragedy.

We have hand curated songs from Indonesia's Gamelan music, and some other types of music, to provide the most cinematic experience out of a puppet show.
