Promote gender equality.

Start promoting gender equality instead of posting offensive posts.


In order to promote gender equality on social media, people should stop making jokes about gender, post comments that discriminate against women, and other forms of discrimination. People should also report discriminatory comments to the social media service.

Additionally, use social media to educate others about gender equality. Post information about gender equality issues, such as equal pay for equal work, sexual harassment, or gender discrimination.

Finally, use social media to find other women who are interested in promoting gender equality. Form groups or communities on social media that are interested in gender equality.
